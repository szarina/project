Contact /help Centers

  1. Immediately contact the police by phone 102 with an oral report of violence or through the portal qamqor.gov.kz submit an online application that must be registered. You have the right to require police officers to go to the scene of domestic violence to take action against the offender.
  2. In the case of injury to the emergency room, number 103. Get a referral from a police officer for a forensic medical examination
  3. ontact the toll-free number 150 or +77081060810 (WhatsApp), website www.telefon150.kz to the National helpline for children, youth and people in situations of violence.
  4. In all regions operated crisis centers for victims of domestic violence. In case of a threat to your life and health, you can apply for shelter in these centers.
  5. In each region, there are commissions for women's Affairs and family and demographic policy, where you can consult and get support.
